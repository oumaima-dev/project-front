Mechanism: We designed the Torshn Puzzle to be as simple as possible to avoid any complications over time. The cavities through which the marbles move are milled from a solid chunk of aluminum. The precision and smoothness of the cavities prevent the marbles from getting worn down, and the sturdiness of the cavities themselves mean that they won't get eroded by glass marbles either. The spinning mechanism of the Torshn Puzzle is made possible by rare earth neodymium magnets, which are permanent magnets and only lose a fraction of their performance every 100 years.
